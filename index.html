Simple Macaroni and Cheese

This easy macaroni and cheese recipe is perfect for any occasion, offering a creamy and delicious dish with minimal ingredients. In just a few simple steps, you can create a comforting meal ideal for family dinners or quick lunches. The recipe combines tender macaroni with a rich cheese sauce, making it a crowd-pleaser for both kids and adults. Whether you're cooking for a weeknight meal or a cozy gathering, this dish is quick, satisfying, and full of flavor. It's a timeless favorite that’s both simple to make and always a hit with anyone who tries it.

Ingredients for Macaroni and Cheese

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese

How to make it:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the macaroni according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, melt the butter and whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Slowly add the milk to the roux, whisking continuously until the sauce thickens.
  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and cook until melted and smooth.
  5. Combine the cooked macaroni and cheese sauce, stirring to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
